Minisymposia

Keynote lectures and invited lectures will be complemented by the following parallel minisymposia organized by recognized experts in targeted research areas and related to all the important topics of the conference.

  • MS-01   Properties of sustainable construction materials (Yonggui CHEN, Sérgio D.N. LOURENÇO)
  • MS-02   Constitutive modeling of geomaterials (Zhongxuan YANG, Fiona C.Y. KWOK)
  • MS-03   Advanced numerical approaches (Pengzhi PAN, Jidong ZHAO)
  • MS-04   Smart geotechnical monitoring (Honghu ZHU, Wanhuan ZHOU, Behzad FATAHI)
  • MS-05   Big data and artificial intelligence in geotechnics (Pin ZHANG, Brian SHEIL)
  • MS-06   Soil-structure interaction (Fuping GAO, Dong-sheng JENG)
  • MS-07   Geo-environmental engineering (Yanjun DU, Kuo TIAN, Manman HU)
  • MS-08   Geo-hazard assessment and mitigation (Daoyuan TAN, Clarence E. CHOI)
  • MS-09   Sustainability and resilience of infrastructures (Dongming ZHANG, Yu WANG)
  • MS-10   Unsaturated soil mechanics (Annan ZHOU, Xiong ZHANG)
  • MS-11   Tunnelling and underground space technology (Louis N.Y. WONG, Qianbing ZHANG)
  • MS-12   Bio-geotechnical engineering (Ningjun JIANG, Anthony Kwan LEUNG)
  • MS-13   Offshore geotechnical engineering (Yinghui TIAN, Ashley P. DYSON)
  • MS-14   Transportation geotechnical engineering (Xianfeng LIU, Jeff Jianfeng WANG)
  • MS-15   Earthquake engineering (Rui WANG, Xiaoqiang GU, Gang WANG)

*The person in parentheses serves as the organizer(s) for each minisymposium. Please note that the organizer is subject to update
